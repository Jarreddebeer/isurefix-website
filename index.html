Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need an iSureFix Maintenance Plan?

Accidents happen, but a broken screen shouldn’t break the bank. With plans to suit your pocket from only R59 p.m., iSureFix defends your beloved phone or tablet device against accidental breakage, at a highly reduced cost.

What type of damage does my warranty cover?

If it’s broken, iFix will fix it*. This includes, but is not limited to: cracked screens, faulty home buttons, faulty microphone, speaker issues, water damage (at iFix’s discretion), or software issues. And if we can’t fix it, we’ll replace devices on the Black Plan. * iSureFix will cover up to three (3) incidents per year.

What do I need to sign up?

  • A South African passport number or ID number
  • The serial numbers of your device(s)
  • Banking details for debit order collection.

To complete your device’s protection, kindly verify ownership of your device by presenting it at your nearest iFix store (you can do this during sign-up).

What are the limitations of iSureFix?

iSureFix only covers up to three (3) incidents per year. Repairs in excess of R300 are liable for a nominal processing fee.

What’s the difference between the Platinum and Black plans?

They offer the same benefits, but only the Black plan @ R99 p.m. has a full device replacement benefit for irreparable devices.

What about my school, business or organisation?

Definitely. iFix can tailor a maintenance plan for any organisations. Please get in touch with our business development team, or email queries@isurefix.co.za.

Duration of Protection

If you pay the monthly fee on time, we’ll fix it. Plans are annually renewable.

Maintenance & Repairs

We commit to do the following, with a maximum of three (3) occurrences during a twelve (12) month period, subject to a processing fee payable by you:

  1. We shall maintain your device in a working condition.
  2. We shall repair your device if damaged, excluding:
    1. Loss or theft of the device.
    2. amage caused by any process of cleaning, dyeing, bleaching, altering, repairing or restoring.
    3. Malicious or purposeful damages.
    4. Damages arising from or contributed to dishonesty on your part.
    5. In the event of liquid damage, repair will be at our sole discretion.
    6. Salt water damage.
    7. Should the damage exceed our ability to repair the device.

In the event of your device not working due to damage defined as the above:

  1. You must report this to an iFix store.
  2. Only an official iFix store may repair and/or work on the device.

Failure to adhere to this, may result in the immediate cancellation of this contract and void any warranty.
